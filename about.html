Our Story

Welcome to Gooey

At Gooey, we believe that dessert should be an experience, not just a treat. Founded by business partners Julia and Antonet, our journey began with a shared obsession for the rich, textural wonder of the viral "Dubai Chocolate". We wanted to bring that same magic to your doorstep—but with our own signature, artisanal twist.

Our Craft

Our craft is defined by a unique fusion of textures, where the traditional crunch of toasted Kataifi meets a soft, pulled marshmallow exterior. We carefully toast the golden shredded pastry in butter to achieve its world-famous snap before enveloping it in a sweet marshmallow shell.

Each sphere is then finished with a dusting of premium cocoa, Biscoff, or Matcha powder to create a multi-layered sensory experience that balances chewiness with an unmistakable crunch.

Our Mission

Our mission is to bring the world-famous textures of Dubai-style Kataifi chocolate to our local community with uncompromising quality and a touch of artisanal flair.

  • Innovate Tradition: Blending classic toasted pastry with modern flavors like Biscoff and Matcha.
  • Celebrate Texture: Ensuring every bite provides that signature "snap" and decadent center.
  • Prioritize Craft: Handcrafting each sphere with premium ingredients for a luxury dessert experience.

Our Vision

Our vision is to become the premier destination for artisanal stuffed treats, recognized globally for our signature marshmallow-clothed Kataifi crunch.

We envision Gooey becoming synonymous with dessert innovation, creating unforgettable moments of pure indulgence.
